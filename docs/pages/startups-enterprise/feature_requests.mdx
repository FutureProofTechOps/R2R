## Feature Requests

We're always looking to improve and expand our offerings, and your input is invaluable to us. If you have a feature request or an idea that you believe would make our product better, we'd love to hear from you!

## How to Submit a Feature Request

### GitHub Issues

For detailed feature requests or if you'd like to track the progress of your request, please open an issue on our GitHub repository:

1. Visit our [Issues tab](https://github.com/SciPhi-AI/R2R/issues)
2. Click on **New Issue** > **Get Started** next to Feature Request.
3. Share some details describing what your proposed feature would help you acomplish and what you've tried so far.
4. Submit your issue.

We regularly review all submissions, and your feedback is crucial for shaping the future of our product.

### Discord Community

For a more informal discussion or to brainstorm ideas with the community, join us on Discord:

1. Join our [Discord server](https://discord.gg/p6KqD2kjtB)
2. Navigate to the **feature-requests** channel.
3. Share your idea or request, and feel free to engage with others.

Our team is active on Discord and will be happy to discuss your suggestions and provide feedback.

Thank you for contributing to the growth and improvement of our product. We're excited to see your ideas and work together to go from RAG(s) to Riches.
